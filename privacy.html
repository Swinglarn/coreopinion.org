Privacy Policy

Your data is yours. Learn how we keep your results anonymous.

At CoreOpinion, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your data when you use our platform.

1. Anonymous Testing

Taking our political compass test is completely anonymous. We do not require you to register for an account, and we do not collect personal identifiers like your name, IP address, or location coordinates. Your answers are stored locally on your device during the test.

2. Optional Demographic Data

Before receiving your results, you may optionally provide quick demographic details (age, gender, nationality). This data is saved in a secure database ONLY if you explicitly check the research consent box. We use this aggregate data solely for statistical research, public polling trends, and platform calibration. It is never linked to individual identities.

3. Result Saving and Sharing

If you choose to use the "Save results" feature, you can enter your email address to receive a permanent link. Your email is used strictly for sending the results link and is stored in a separate table. We do not share, sell, or rent your email address to third parties, and we will never send you marketing messages.

4. Cookies and Analytics

We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies. We may collect basic, anonymous server-side traffic statistics to ensure platform stability and performance. No personal profiling is done.

5. GDPR and Rights

If you wish to delete any saved results associated with your email address, or if you have any questions regarding privacy, please contact us at privacy@webbitar.com. We fully comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and respect your right to data erasure.
