About

A political test built differently.

Most political compass tests ask you whether you agree or disagree with a list of statements. CoreOpinion is different. Every question is a scenario or a real decision, the kind of trade-off that actually shapes political opinion.

The test also tracks something most people don't expect. Some beliefs are tested twice, once framed one way and once the other. If your answers shift with the framing, that gap becomes your bias score. It is revealed only at the end.

100
Questions across 25 political topics in the full test
3

How it works
1
Choose your length
Short (30), medium (60), or long (100) questions. Longer gives more precision.
2
Answer the questions
Each question is a scenario or a statement. Pick the answer that reflects your real view.
3
Get two results
Your political position on the compass, and a framing bias score that reveals how consistent your thinking really is.

CoreOpinion is free and anonymous. No sign-up required. Your answers are never linked to your identity unless you choose to save your results by email.
