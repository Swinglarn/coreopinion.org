About CoreOpinion

Discover the vision behind our policy-focused political compass test.

CoreOpinion is a next-generation political compass platform created to foster political self-awareness and critical thinking. Rather than relying purely on abstract, survey-style questions (such as "Governments should tax the rich"), we present a balanced mix of direct policy questions and core ideological statements. You select the stance that closest aligns with your view, which maps directly to real-world party platforms and policy details.

Our Mission

In a highly polarized political landscape, we believe that understanding *why* you agree or disagree with a political stance is just as important as the stance itself. Our platform aims to clarify these alignments through clean data and objective analysis, without corporate or institutional bias.

The Framing Bias Engine

A central feature of CoreOpinion is our cognitive framing bias engine. By pairing identical policy issues with different rhetorical framing (e.g., support vs. critical consequences), we measure how sensitive your political alignments are to emotional phrasing. We believe this insight is crucial for navigating modern media and political messaging.

Who We Are

CoreOpinion is built by a small team of independent political researchers, software developers, and designers. We do not receive funding from any political party, candidate, or lobbying group. The platform is completely free to use and respects user privacy above all else.
